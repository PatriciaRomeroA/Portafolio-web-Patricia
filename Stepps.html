Stepps

This project involves developing an application aimed at businesses, where they can subscribe to provide access to mental health support resources for all their employees.

Corporate Subscription: System allowing companies to register and grant access to specialized mental health content to all employees.

Supportive Content: Offers resources designed to enhance employees' emotional and psychological well-being, including tools and educational materials.

Accessibility and Usability: Intuitive interface facilitating navigation and content access, ensuring a user-friendly experience.
